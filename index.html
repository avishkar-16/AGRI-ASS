USES OF THE ASHOKA TREE

  • The bark is valued in Ayurveda for treating menstrual disorders and uterine problems.
  • Flower extracts are used to address skin ailments and internal bleeding.
  • Contains anti-inflammatory compounds helpful in relieving joint pain.
  • Serves as an ornamental plant in gardens, parks, and avenues for its vivid flowers.
